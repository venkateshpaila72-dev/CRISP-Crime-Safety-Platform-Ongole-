STOP CYBERBULLYING

The internet is awesome — let's keep it a safe and happy place for everyone!
💬 "Would I say this to their face?"
1 WHAT IS CYBERBULLYING?
📱😢
Cyberbullying is when someone uses the internet, apps, or phones to hurt, scare, or embarrass another person on purpose — over and over again.
2 TYPES YOU SHOULD KNOW
🔥
Flaming
Angry, rude messages sent to start a fight
🎭
Impersonation
Pretending to be someone else online
🙈
Exclusion
Leaving someone out on purpose
🗯️
Trolling
Posting mean comments to upset people
📤
Outing
Sharing someone's secrets or private pics
🕵️
Stalking
Repeatedly messaging someone who wants space
3 SIGNS A FRIEND MIGHT BE BULLIED
✓Suddenly avoids their phone or laptop
✓Looks upset after going online
✓Doesn't want to go to school
✓Becomes quiet or stops talking to friends
✓Trouble sleeping or eating
✓Deletes their social media accounts
4 WHAT CAN YOU DO?
1
✋
Don't reply or fight back
2
📸
Save proof (screenshot)
3
🚫
Block & report the bully
4
🗣️
Tell a trusted adult
5
💛
Support the victim, don't watch silently
